Productive vs. idle time by driver

Hours by driver

From RouteGenie shift telemetry (Mar 16 – Jun 6, 2026). Productive = Loaded (passenger onboard) + En-route (driving to pickup). Idle = RouteGenie "Wait" time (on the clock, not moving a passenger or en route). Utilization = Productive ÷ (Productive + Idle), shaded red (low) → green (high).
