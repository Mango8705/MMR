Per-driver breakdown

Direct cost = Pay (payroll) + Fuel (fleet card) + Insurance ($9,100/mo at $350/operating-day). Excludes overhead (office payroll, software, fees, etc.), so “Rev − cost” (green positive, red/parentheses negative) is not net profit. Click any header to sort high→low or low→high.

Revenue vs. direct cost by broker

Per-broker profitability

Broker = payer on each trip. Direct cost is allocated from the Financials tab: each driver’s pay+fuel+insurance is split across the brokers they served, in proportion to trips. “Rev − cost” = green when the broker covers its allocated cost, red/parentheses when it doesn’t. Excludes overhead, so it is not net profit. Click any header to sort.

Revenue vs. direct cost by level of service

Per-service profitability

Level of service = trip mode (Ambulatory, Wheelchair, Stretcher). Direct cost is allocated from the Financials tab by each driver's trips. "Rev - cost" / Margin show profitability per service level; excludes overhead, so not net profit. Click any header to sort.
